G1 Ready - Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

G1 Ready ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Ontario G1 driver's test preparation app handles your information.

Information We Collect

G1 Ready stores all data locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

Data stored locally on your device includes:

  • Quiz scores and progress
  • XP points and achievement badges
  • Learning mode preferences
  • Exam date settings
  • Bookmarked questions

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name or email address
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not collect device identifiers
  • We do not share any data with third parties
  • We do not use analytics or tracking

Children Privacy

G1 Ready is intended for users aged 16 and older who are preparing for their Ontario G1 driver's test. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Data Security

All app data is stored locally on your device using Android's secure storage. We have no access to this data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
